About me

¡Hello and welcome!

I'm Yonathan Cortés, a passionate 3D modeler who is taking his first steps into the world of web development. Currently, I'm immersed in the exciting task of creating my own website from scratch.

Although my experience in web development is limited, I'm dedicating time and effort to learn the fundamentals and put them into practice in this personal project. Every line of code and every visual element I incorporate into my website is an opportunity to learn and grow in this field.

In my portfolio, you'll find a showcase of my work in 3D modeling, as well as a glimpse into my learning process in web development through my under-construction page. I'm excited to share this journey with you and demonstrate how I'm applying my 3D modeling skills in the context of web development.

If you're interested in following my progress closely or want to chat about design and development, feel free to get in touch with me!

Thank you for visiting my online portfolio and being part of my adventure in web development.
